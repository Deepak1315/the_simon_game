• Play
  • Rules
  • Click any key to start the game. A button will blink.
  • To pass "Level 1", you have to click that button
  • If it is correct another button will blink.
  • To pass "Level 2", you have to remember the button from "Level 1" and click on those 2 buttons in that order.
  • As the game progresses, you have to remember the before appeared sequence and click in that order to reach further level.
